Derivatives of some familiar functions

The definition above doesn't give us many tools for actually computing derivatives; it appears as though we'd need a magic “limit-computing” operation to do so. Nonetheless, we can build up an arsenal of results about derivatives that allow us to differentiate many familiar functions.
