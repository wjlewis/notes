The extended reals

Compactness

We can generalize the previous result to extend the Bolzano-Weierstrass theorem to the entire extended real line: every sequence in R‾\overline{\mathbb{R}}R has a convergent subsequence. This is just another way of saying that R‾\overline{\mathbb{R}}R is sequentially compact.
